How to Use This Calculator

Enter your loan amount, interest rate and term to see your minimum monthly repayment. Then try adding extra monthly payments to see how dramatically they reduce your interest and payoff time.

💡 Quick Tip

Even an extra $200/month on a $600k loan at 6.2% saves over $80,000 in interest and cuts nearly 5 years off your loan.
