Fortnightly vs Monthly — Why It Matters

Switching from monthly to fortnightly repayments is one of the easiest ways to save tens of thousands in interest. Here's why it works:

💡 The Maths

There are 26 fortnights in a year but only 12 months. Paying half your monthly repayment fortnightly means you make the equivalent of 13 monthly payments per year — one extra payment every year, automatically.
